Improving Bitcoin UX
Insights from Bitcoin++ Conference

Enhancing the usability of P2P Bitcoin trading with Mostro

Introduction

Designing for Bitcoin isn't just about aesthetics—
it's about creating seamless, intuitive experiences that empower users.

At the Bitcoin++ Conference and Bitcoin UX Bootcamp in Florianópolis, I had the chance to put this philosophy into action.

As part of the event, I collaborated with other UX designers to improve Mostro, a P2P Bitcoin exchange leveraging Lightning & Nostr for fast, private, and decentralized transactions.

Our goal? To refine the user experience and make Bitcoin trading smoother.

Usability Testing Insights

To identify friction points in the Mostro mobile app, we conducted usability tests with real users.

Key Findings & Takeaways

While Mostro impressed with strong fundamentals, testers flagged key pain points around navigation, payment inputs, and order creation.
